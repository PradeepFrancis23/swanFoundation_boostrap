Voiceless Animals. It’ll blow your mind.

According to the Voiceless Animal Cruelty Index (VACI), which ranks the animal welfare performance of 50 countries that are responsible for almost 80% of the world’s farmed animal production, India ranks 13th, while Australia ranks 41st1. The VACI considers factors such as the number and types of animals farmed, the intensity of farming, the legal protection of animals, and the consumption of animal products1.
